About TwTube

The TwTube plugin brings the Video.js Video Player plus some player skins to TiddlyWiki 5. Simpy install the player plugin, and optionally a skin plugin or some more Video.js player plugins.

  • Skin plugins
    • iPlayer-inspired player skin
    • Sublime-inspired player skin
    • YouTube-inspired player skin
  • Videojs plugins
    • persistent volume player plugin
    • hotkeys player plugin

You can even multiple skin plugins simultaneously, but then make sure to only enable on skin plugin at a time. Otherwise, your video player may look and behave slightly odd.
